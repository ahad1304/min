Recipe #1

  • Time: Around 10 minutes
  • Serves: A wonderful fry egg.
Ingredients Quantity
Egg As much as you want.
Oil One table spoon.
Pan One.
A source of fire As much as needed.
Salt 1/6 of tea spoon.

Step 1: First, take your egg and brake it and take the egg into a plate.

Step 2:Give salt into the plate and bland it.

Step 3:Give salt into the plate and blend it.

Step 4:Take the pan and put it into the source of fire for around 5 minutes giving oil into the pan.

Step 5:Then put the egg into the pan and wait until it is fried. After that, your egg is ready to eat.
